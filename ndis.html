NDIS Information

Understanding how your support can work

Simple information to help you make confident choices.

Plan-managed

Your plan manager receives invoices and pays providers on your behalf.

Self-managed

You choose providers and manage payments directly from your NDIS budget.

NDIA-managed

Registered providers can claim directly through the NDIS portal for approved supports.

Changing providers

You have choice and control over who delivers your supports. Review your current service agreement, notice period and outstanding bookings, then speak with your coordinator or plan manager when needed.
