FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Clear answers about getting started with iHelp Care Services.

Do you offer a free consultation?

Yes. We offer a friendly, no-obligation consultation of up to two hours.

Which areas do you service?

We support participants across the Gold Coast, including Coomera, Upper Coomera, Helensvale, Southport and nearby suburbs.

Can you provide transport?

Yes. Transport can be arranged as part of your support, subject to your plan and service agreement.

Do you support plan-managed and self-managed participants?

Yes. Please contact us to discuss your funding arrangement and service needs.

Do you offer overnight support?

Overnight, sleepover and 24-hour arrangements may be available after assessment and planning.

What languages do you speak?

Support is available in English, Mandarin, Cantonese and conversational Japanese.

How quickly can support start?

Start times depend on your needs, location, worker matching and current capacity. Contact us for availability.
